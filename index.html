About This Tool

This Chinese text annotation tool uses the CC-CEDICT dictionary with 120,000+ entries, CVDICT Chinese-Vietnamese dictionary with 122,000+ entries, and real HSK 2.0/3.0 vocabulary data to help you learn Chinese by:

  • Adding spaces between words for easier reading
  • Displaying inline Pinyin or other phonetic systems above characters
  • Showing pop-up definitions in English and/or Vietnamese when you hover over words
  • Generating vocabulary lists filtered by actual HSK level (1-6+)
  • Advanced word segmentation for accurate word boundaries

Dictionaries: CC-CEDICT by MDBG (CC BY-SA 4.0 License) and CVDICT by Phong Phan (CC BY-SA 4.0 License)
